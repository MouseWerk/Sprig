From spreadsheet to studied in three steps

No deck editors, no cloud sync, no signup walls. If you can write two columns, you can study.

Write a CSV

Export from Excel, Google Sheets, or ask any AI to generate one. Each row is question,answer — that’s the whole format.

Import it into Sprig

Pick the file and it becomes a deck instantly. The CSV stays the source of truth — update the file, and the deck updates too.

Study your way

Swipe with spaced repetition, quiz yourself with multiple choice, or scroll cards like a feed. Sprig schedules reviews so you see the hard cards more and the easy ones less.

Everything you need to actually study

Six tools, one quiet app. No ads, no upsells, no distractions.

Swipe to learn

SM-2 spaced repetition decides when each card comes back. Right if you knew it, left if you didn’t.

Quiz mode

Multiple-choice questions generated from your own deck, with your weak cards showing up more often.

Feed mode

Scroll question and answer cards like a social feed — study during the scrolling you were going to do anyway.

Focus garden

Start a focus session and a plant grows while you stay in the app. Leave too long and it wilts. Don’t let it wilt.

Ambient sounds

Rain, campfire, forest, ocean — mix your own background while you study or focus.

Stats & streaks

Daily streaks, levels, and achievements track the habit — not just the hours.

Nothing leaves your phone

Sprig has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. Your decks, stats, and audio live in a local database on your device — delete the app and they’re gone. That’s the whole privacy policy, but you can read the long version too.

  • Works fully offline
  • No account, ever
  • No tracking or analytics
  • Your files stay your files

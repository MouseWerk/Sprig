Sprig — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

The short version: Sprig stores everything on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking of any kind.

What data Sprig handles

Sprig is a study app for flashcards, PDF documents, and focus sessions. The content you add — flashcard decks, PDF files, audio files, study statistics (streaks, experience points, achievements), and app preferences — is stored only in the app's private storage on your device. None of it is ever uploaded to a server, because Sprig has no server.

What Sprig does not do

  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No analytics or crash-reporting services
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No transmission of your content or usage data to anyone, including us
  • No third-party data sharing

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Sprig schedules local reminders (for example a daily study reminder or a focus-session alert) directly on your device. These never leave your phone. You can disable them at any time in the app's settings or in your system settings.

Files you import

When you import a CSV, PDF, or audio file, Sprig copies it into its own private storage so your decks keep working even if the original file moves. These copies stay on your device and are deleted when you delete the item or uninstall the app.

Backups

The optional backup feature creates a file containing your decks and statistics and hands it to the sharing destination you choose (e.g. your own cloud drive). Sprig never sends backups anywhere on its own.

Permissions

Sprig requests only the permissions it needs: network access is required by the underlying framework, vibration powers optional haptic feedback, and notification permission enables the optional reminders described above. Sprig does not use the microphone, camera, or your location.

Children

Sprig does not collect data from anyone, including children. The app is intended for general audiences (students aged 13 and up).
